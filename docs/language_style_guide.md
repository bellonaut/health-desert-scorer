# Health Desert Scorer - Language Guidelines

## Avoid (Deficit or Blame Framing)
- "Worst LGAs"
- "Poor health outcomes"
- "Failing areas"
- "Underperforming regions"
- "Problem areas"

## Use (Barrier or Systems Framing)
- "Highest-need LGAs"
- "Access barriers"
- "Under-resourced areas"
- "Priority for support"
- "Facing systemic constraints"

## Examples

### Risk score labels
- Avoid: "This LGA has poor access"
- Use: "This LGA faces significant access barriers"

### Rankings
- Avoid: "Bottom 10 LGAs"
- Use: "10 LGAs with highest access barriers"

### Recommendations
- Avoid: "This area needs fixing"
- Use: "Priority for infrastructure investment and mobile outreach"

### Data quality
- Avoid: "Bad data quality"
- Use: "Limited data availability - interpret with caution"
